How it Works

SECURE OFFLINE STORAGE

This tracker operates completely isolated in your browser. No cloud databases are used. Warning: If you clear your browser cache/cookies, your tracking data will be lost. Use the Backup Data button in Analytics often to save your progress locally.

Features

  • Drag & Drop: Reorder your habits by clicking their headers and dragging.
  • Custom Colors: Click the palette icon next to a habit to theme it.
  • Streaks: Keep unbroken checkmarks to ignite streak flames!
  • Infinite Grid: Simply scroll down. Once you approach Day 30, the grid infinitely generates new days.
  • Lifetime Analytics: The heatmap and tracking distributions scale automatically to track your entire lifetime.
  • Daily Journal: The massive right column is for your end-of-day thoughts.
